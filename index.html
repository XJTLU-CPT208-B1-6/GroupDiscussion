XJTLU Group Discussions

CPT208 Course Group Project | Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University

Project Theme

This project is dedicated to fostering more meaningful, in-depth, and equitable group discussions among XJTLU students in academic and language classrooms.

We aim to build a platform that balances participation, aligns conversations with learning objectives, and encourages critical thinking across multicultural teams.
